What Is CTE?

The C.T.E. program stands for Career Technical Education, which involves the
Six District Compact, a series of schools who offer vocational programs similar to each other.

What Schools are Involved?

Currently, only six school districts are active participants in this program. These include:

  • Cuyahoga Falls School District
  • Hudson School District
  • Stow-Munroe Falls School District
  • Kent City School District
  • Woodridge School District
  • Tallmage School District
