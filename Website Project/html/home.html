Basic Overview

In a nutshell, the Electronics, Robotics, and Programming course aims to teach students the fundamentals needed to get a job in these fields. As a Level 1 student, you will be introduced to the basics of programming an industrial robot, soldering techniques, Ohms Law, parallel and series circuit, and much more.
